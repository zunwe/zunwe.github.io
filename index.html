Introduction to Myself

Growing Up

I was born in Burma and grew up in the States. I remembered the struggle to fit in. It took around a year for me to truly get used to living in another coutry.

Growing up in the States made me really think about my identity, ethnicity, and nationality. These topics that used to confuse me as other people decide what I am. A part of growing up is you get to know more about youself, something I'm still doing.

Currently

As I step into the adulthood, there are still so many things I'm unsure about. After highschool is college and after college should be a job. But, what should I do if I don't even know what I want to be in the future?

Being an adult sure is terrifying when you're unsure about your future. There have probably been so many young adult before me who felt this and now doing just fine. I hope one day I too will know what I want to do in life.

This is the introduction of myself. Which includes the problems I had growing up and the problems as a person who is in their early adulthood. And for anyone that is feeling the same as I am, I wish you the best of luck!
